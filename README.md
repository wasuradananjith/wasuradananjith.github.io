## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a dedicated software engineer and researcher with a First Class Honours BSc in Computer Science, currently pursuing my PhD at the University of New South Wales (UNSW) Canberra. Previously, I worked as a Senior Software Engineer at WSO2, contributing to enterprise-level products and CI/CD tooling.

* 🔭 **Currently working on:** My PhD research focusing on Human-Computer Interaction, Swarm Intelligence, and Multi-Agent Systems.

* 🎓 **Teaching:** Casual Academic (Lab Demonstrator & Tutor) at UNSW Canberra for Programming, Computer Technology, and Computer Games courses.

* 🌱 **Research Interests:** HCI, Human-Swarm Interaction, AI/Machine Learning, Affective Computing, and Serious Games.

* 🏆 **Recent Awards:** Arc PGC Research Candidate Award 2024, People's Choice Award (3MT Finals), HDR Engagement Award.

* 💬 **Ask me about:** Java, Go, API Management, Academic Research, or organizing student community events!
